The Founder

I, Siddhant Goel, on starting my coding journey in grade 9, I was very confused and found it difficult to navigate through the webbed world of coding. It was very strenuous to find the order to learn programming languages and find the appropriate partners to work on projects.

Hence to ease the strain on budding developers I created this community to assist people to find the right way to start their coding journey and help them connect with the right people to collaborate with.
